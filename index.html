Welcome to the new herizon on my OS!

Today I am presenting to you a spaceship system, like something out of a science fiction movie. My nickname is trynkos. I am a die-hard space fanatic and rope-climbing lover. The most marvelous fact about the universe that I grew up with is that no sound travels through the galaxy — not even from a massive explosion!

My inspiration character is Ryland Grace from Project Hail Mary. He was mostly overlooked in his life, but he didn't care to voice his judgment, no matter how stupid it sounded to outstanding people. He was alone and mostly kept to himself. Still, he had 30 beloved students to take care of in his teaching. Just an all-around happy, outstanding man with a peaceful vision, ...until some people had to destroy it for selfish reasons. His opinion didn't matter to them, only how useful he was to them. That pretty much sums up my perspective on humanity in this world.

That is enough nerdy stuff. Thanks for reading! Have fun in my personal OS!
